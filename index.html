Farming on Crutches is an initiative of the Single Leg Amputee Sports Association (SLASA).

We have developed a 3-acre demonstration farm.

We teach regenerative agriculture practices and techniques through a hands-on Permaculture Design Course.

We enable participants to return to their communities to grow their own food or set up sustainable food growing enterprises.
